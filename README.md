# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes workforce data containing 50,000 employee records to uncover employee attrition trends, workforce demographics, job satisfaction patterns, and retention opportunities.

The objective of the project is to help HR teams and business leaders understand the factors influencing employee turnover and support data-driven workforce decisions.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Excel
- SQL

---

## Dataset Size

- Total Employees: 50,000

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Workforce Overview

Provides a high-level summary of workforce demographics, department distribution, employee counts, salary trends, and attrition metrics.

### 2. Employee Attrition Analysis

Explores attrition across:

- Departments
- Job Roles
- Marital Status
- Business Travel
- Distance from Home
- Overtime

### 3. Employee Experience & Satisfaction

Analyzes:

- Job Satisfaction
- Environment Satisfaction
- Work-Life Balance
- Salary Distribution
- Attrition Drivers

---

## Key Insights

### Distance from Home is a Major Attrition Driver

Employees living more than 20 KM away experienced the highest attrition levels, suggesting commuting challenges may contribute to employee turnover.

### Attrition is Organization-Wide

Attrition levels remain consistently high across departments, indicating broader organizational retention challenges.

### Overtime Shows Limited Impact

Attrition rates are nearly identical between employees working overtime and those who do not, suggesting other factors play a larger role.

### Gender and Marital Status Show Minimal Influence

Employee turnover is distributed relatively evenly across demographic groups.

### Compensation Alone Does Not Explain Attrition

Attrition exists across multiple salary ranges, indicating retention challenges extend beyond pay considerations.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Introduce hybrid or remote work options where feasible.
- Improve employee engagement and retention initiatives.
- Investigate career progression and development opportunities.
- Conduct deeper workforce analytics to identify hidden attrition drivers.
- Monitor high-risk employee groups through ongoing HR reporting.
